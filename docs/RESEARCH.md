# LegalTech Market Research - India

## 1. Market Overview
- **Market Size:** $3-5 Billion in Legal Services (estimated).
- **Opportunity:** The Indian legal system has a massive backlog. Access to justice and fast document review is highly demanded by SMEs.
- **Competitors:**
  - Traditional Law Firms (Expensive, slow)
  - Existing LegalTech (Manupatra, SCC Online - mostly search engines, lacking AI analysis)
  - Harvey.ai (Too expensive for Indian SMEs)

## 2. SWOT Analysis
### Strengths
- Fast AI responses
- Cost-effective for SMEs
- Accessible via API

### Weaknesses
- Data privacy concerns
- Trust in AI answers

### Opportunities
- Millions of SMEs need basic legal review
- Massive open-source Indian legal dataset (IndianKanoon)

### Threats
- Changes in AI regulations
- Competition from established legal databases adding AI

## 3. Product Strategy
- Focus on Employment and B2B Contracts first.
- Price competitively for Indian SMEs (e.g., INR 9,999/month).
- Ensure 100% data privacy and compliance with DPDP Act.

## 4. Technical Strategy
- RAG using open-source embeddings (BGE-M3).
- Claude 3.5 Sonnet for complex reasoning.
- Supabase for fast pgvector deployment.

## 5. Conclusion
There is a clear market gap for a fast, affordable AI legal assistant targeted at Indian SMEs. By leveraging open data and modern LLMs, we can achieve break-even rapidly.
